Why Bali

Bali, July 2023

There is raw beauty all over and around Bali. The island boasts a myriad of impressive volcanoes, crystalline beaches, world-class diving, diverse nightlife, iconic ricefields, and and gorgeous waterfalls. Breathtaking hiking can be found all over the island, as well as on the nearby Lombok and Java islands.

Bali is the most well-known destination among Indonesia’s 17,000 islands. We love that it offers something for everyone, whether you’re looking to chill out on the beach, find your zen in the many yoga studios, or delve into the island’s art scene. There’s also plenty to do for adventurous souls, be it hiking volcanoes at sunrise or swimming in waterfalls at sunset.
